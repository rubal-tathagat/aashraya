Clinic Facility

Our dedicated staff of doctors have striven to provide an efficient and reliable space for pet owners to take their furry companions for treatment. On top of providing reliable treatment the clinic is also one the least expensive in the city making it a popular destination for pet owners.
Our team of medical attenders are well trained and efficient and can handle minor and cosmetic procedures while the doctors focus on the more serious concerns. The clinic also provides the options of housing and handling post-operative care for those animals whose owners would not be able to manage such situations at home.
