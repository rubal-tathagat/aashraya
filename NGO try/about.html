About Us

आSHRAYA is a nonprofit/ non-government organization. The Indian Constitution in its Article 51A establishes that it is the duty of every citizen of India "To protect and improve the natural environment including forests, lakes, rivers, and wildlife, and to have compassion for living creatures". आSHRAYA vision is deeply rooted in this dictum. आSHRAYA aims to prevent cruelty to animals and protect them from injury, to promote animal care, welfare and foster the human-animal relationship.
It envisions an India where people treat each other with empathy, animals with compassion and care for the environment. So as to prevent cruelty to all living beings

We accomplish our mission by:
Conducting humane education workshops, painting, essay writing competetions, organizing awareness talks and other community outreach programs.
Running a very active animal helpline that organizes rescue of animals in distress; helps in adoption of abandoned pets and other animal welfare activities.
There is also round the clock ambulance service for the strays as well as an emergency night clinic for pets.
